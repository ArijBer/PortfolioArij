The Making of Quizzzy Trivia Game

Unveiling the Quiz App: Where Technology Meets Design and Knowledge Unfolds!

To develop this exciting Quizzzy Game, we utilized Android Studio as our primary software, leveraging the prowess of both Kotlin and Java programming languages to build a seamless and intuitive user experience.

We employed Figma and Photoshop to craft stunning visuals, ensuring a visually appealing and user-friendly interface. The combination of these powerful tools allowed us to bring our creative vision to reality and enhance the overall user engagement.

Behind the scenes, we employed SQLite DB as our robust database solution, seamlessly managing vast amounts of trivia questions and user data, ensuring smooth and efficient performance.
